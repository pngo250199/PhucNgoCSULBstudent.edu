ABOUT ME

My name is Phuc (Henry) Ngo, graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at Cal State University Long Beach. I am deeply passionate about the field of Embedded Systems. Throughout my academic journey and personal projects, I have actively explored and developed microcontroller-based systems, gaining hands-on experience in robotics and the Internet of Things (IoT).

My interests extend to firmware development, with notable projects involving drones and robotic platforms. Currently, I am delving into Embedded AI, applying TinyML techniques on microcontrollers such as the ESP32, STM32, and Arduino Uno. My most ambitious project to date is the development of an autonomous drone, which integrates my skills in embedded systems, firmware, and AI.
